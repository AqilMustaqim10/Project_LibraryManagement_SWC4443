Frequently Asked Questions

How do I borrow books from the library?

To borrow books, you need to become a registered member of the library. Once registered, you can borrow books using your library card.

How many books can I borrow at once?

The number of books you can borrow at once is only one (1)

How do I return books?

You can return books by dropping them off at the library's return desk during opening hours. Alternatively, you can use the book drop-off box located outside the library for after-hours returns.
